Why We Are Raising Funds

The Trendsetters Ministry is raising funds to sponsor 15 young people to attend the 2026 National Trendsetters Conference.


This conference promotes spiritual growth, leadership development, fellowship, unity and service.


Our goal is to ensure that financial challenges do not prevent young people from attending this life-changing experience.
